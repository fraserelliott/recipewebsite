Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 50g parmesan cheese, shaved
  • 100g croutons
  • 2 tbsp Caesar dressing
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. In a large bowl, toss romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing and garlic.
  2. Add croutons and parmesan shavings.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve immediately.
