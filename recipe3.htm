Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt
  • Maple syrup (to serve)

Method

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat, pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
  6. Serve with maple syrup.
