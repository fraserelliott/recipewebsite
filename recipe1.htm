Spaghetti and Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 250g minced beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil, chopped (for garnish)

Method

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a bowl, mix minced beef, egg, breadcrumbs, and half the garlic; form into small meatballs.
  3. Fry the meatballs until browned.
  4. In a pan, saut√© onion and remaining garlic in olive oil, then add canned tomatoes.
  5. Simmer sauce for 15 minutes, add meatballs, and cook for 10 more minutes.
  6. Serve over spaghetti, garnished with basil.
