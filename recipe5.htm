Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Fresh coriander, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Mash the avocados in a bowl.
  2. Stir in lime juice, onion, tomato, garlic, and coriander.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Mix well and serve fresh with tortilla chips or as a dip.
